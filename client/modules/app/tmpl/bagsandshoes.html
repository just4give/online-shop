Resonsive website design

Resonsive website which means your online store can be viewed on iPhone, iPad, Android Phone, Tables as well as desktops. Your online store will render on different devices differently.

Customizable HTML templates

Bags & Shoes is just a sample online store backed by powerful REST APIs. That means HTML can be customized easily as per your business requirements while reusing already built and tested backend code. And your business can be reachable to customers in few days.

SEO friendly url

Google search engine friendly urls makes sure your web pages are indexed by Google and appear on google search page. SEO friendly urls are excellent for book marking and easy to remember by human brains.

Rich Product Search Capability

Rich and interactive product search experience loaded with facet search to narrow down search result. In built pagination, sorting.

Easy sign-up with Facebook

Online customers are lazy and even lazier when they are asked to create profile typing a bunch of information. This app integrates facebook login to help buyers sign-up quickly.

Integrated Email templates

Built-in email templates which send emails to buyers when they sign-up, place order, order is shipped. Admin receives emails when any order is placed or any refund/cancel is requested.

Secured payments using Paypal SDK

We use paypal SDK to capture buyers payment securely. Your application can accept paypal payments as well as any visa and master card.

Easy refund and cancel processing

Built-in order cancel and refund processing. Buyer can request cancel or refund online and admin can process the request from admin panel. System sends email confirmation to buyers when request is processed.

Buyers Orders

Buyers can view and track their orders online. Buyers receive emails when order is placed and shipped. Buyers can cancel entire order or individual items online from 'My Orders' page.

Animated graphical admin panel

Every time you login to admin panel, you will see your sales trend and most selling products graphically. Dashboard will also indicate if any new order or refund requests were placed.

Configurable product catalog

You as admin can configure your entire catalog. You can add new merchandise, new tax scheme, new shipping methods. You can review all new orders, refund requests and process them.

Promotional coupons and Marketing

Promo and discounts are integral part of successful marketing of your business. We have in-built mechanism to create promo codes and discounts which buyers can apply during checkout.
